BABY MONSTER

BabyMonster (Korean: 베이비몬스터; stylized in all caps), also known as Baemon (Korean: 베몬), is an upcoming South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment. Consisting of seven members—Ruka, Pharita, Asa, Ahyeon, Haram, Rora, and Chiquita—the septet released a pre-debut single entitled "Dream", on May 14, 2023.

SEVEN

Choi Dong Wook (Korean: 최동욱, born November 9, 1984), better known by his stage name Se7en (pronounced "seven", Korean: 세븐), is a South Korean male singer by genre. pop and R&B. Se7en joined YG Entertainment at the age of 15 and has been practicing ever since. After 4 years of vocal and dance training, Se7en achieved its first success in 2003 and spread to Asia.

JINUSEAN

Jinusean (Hangul: 지누션; stylized as JINUSEAN) was a South Korean hip hop duo signed to YG Entertainment. The duo, which is made up of Kim Jin-woo (also known as Jinu) and Noh Seung-hwan (also known as Sean), debuted in 1997 and rose to fame with the single, "Tell Me." They are considered pioneers of Korean hip hop.
